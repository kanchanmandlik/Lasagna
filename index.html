🍝 Classic Italian Lasagna

Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese.

Prep Time

30 mins

Cook Time

1 hour

Servings

8 People

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 800g tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 300g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the parmesan cheese.
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up.
  10. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  11. Bake for 40-45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  12. Rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

💡 Chef's Tips

For the best flavor, prepare the meat sauce a day ahead. Let the lasagna rest after baking to help the layers set properly.
